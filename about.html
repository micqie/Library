About Our Library System

Welcome to the Library Management System

Our library system is designed to provide a seamless and efficient experience for students and staff at the College of Computing and Information Technologies. We offer a modern approach to library management with features that make accessing and managing library resources easier than ever.

Key Features

  • Digital Library Card with QR Code
  • Automated Visit Tracking
  • Real-time Library Status
  • Easy Profile Management
  • Visit History Tracking

Our Mission

To provide an efficient, user-friendly library management system that enhances the learning experience of our students and supports the academic goals of our institution.
